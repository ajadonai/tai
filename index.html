About TAI Advisory

A Policy & Research Organization with a Global Justice Lens

Bridging Technology, Law, and Human Rights

Founded by Chukwuebuka Ogbodo — a Harvard Law-trained attorney, Google Policy Fellow, HBS MBA candidate, and former Meta policy lead — TAI Advisory bridges the gap between technology, law, and human rights. We specialize in advising on AI governance and digital rights through cutting-edge, data-informed strategies, amplifying underrepresented voices in tech policy and working across borders, cultures, and legal systems to advance equitable technology frameworks.

Our Approach

Theory of Change

We believe just technology requires three foundations: informed policy, inclusive research, and empowered communities.

01

Research & Analyze

We produce rigorous, independent research on AI systems, digital rights, and platform governance — centering perspectives from the Global South and marginalized communities.

02

We work directly with policymakers, companies, and multilateral bodies to craft regulation and governance frameworks that are fair, transparent, and technically grounded.

03

Build & Empower

What We Offer

Core Advisory Services

We help organizations navigate complex regulatory environments, build responsible AI frameworks, and ensure their technologies are safe, fair, and transparent.

AI Governance & Regulatory Strategy

Ensure compliance with evolving AI laws like the EU AI Act and U.S. executive orders. We develop tailored internal governance frameworks and provide comprehensive risk assessment.

Digital Rights & Content Policy

Design effective platform governance and content moderation strategies while ensuring privacy and data protection compliance. Navigate cross-border digital rights challenges.

Trust & Safety Strategy

Identify, assess, and mitigate algorithmic bias, develop inclusive AI frameworks, and actively engage stakeholders to build lasting trust across your AI systems.

Policy Research & Thought Leadership

Produce insightful white papers, policy briefs, and custom research to inform strategic decisions and position your organization as a leader in AI governance.
