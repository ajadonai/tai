# TAI Advisory

**Advancing Justice in Technology, Governance, and Digital Rights**

TAI Advisory is a U.S.-based policy and research organization at the intersection of AI governance, digital rights, and public interest technology.

## Live Site

https://ajadonai.github.io/tai/

## Stack

- Pure HTML/CSS/JS — zero dependencies, no build step
- Newsreader (serif) + Manrope (sans) typography
- Warm ivory palette with forest teal accent
- Fully responsive: mobile, tablet, desktop
- Safe viewport handling (dvh, safe-area-inset, -webkit-fill-available)

## SEO & Technical

- Open Graph + Twitter Card meta tags
- JSON-LD structured data (Organization schema)
- `sitemap.xml` and `robots.txt`
- Custom `404.html` matching site design
- Semantic HTML5 landmarks
- `prefers-reduced-motion` accessibility support
- Social share image (`og-image.png`)

## Email Capture

- Mailchimp integration ready (JSONP)
- Two capture points: CTA section + newsletter section
- Setup: replace `YOUR_MAILCHIMP_FORM_ACTION_URL` in index.html with your Mailchimp embedded form URL

## Files

- `index.html` — Complete single-page site
- `sitemap.xml` — XML sitemap
- `robots.txt` — Crawler directives
- `404.html` — Custom error page
- `og-image.png` — Social share image (1200x630)
